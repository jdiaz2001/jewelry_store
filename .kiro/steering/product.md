# Product

A bilingual (Spanish/English) jewelry catalog website for a small retail store. Displays product cards with images, descriptions, reference codes, and prices in a responsive grid layout.

## Features

- Hero banner with gradient background and call-to-action
- Sticky header with store branding, navigation, social links (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok), WhatsApp contact button, and language toggle
- Sidebar category filters (Aretes, Dijes, Anillos) with product counts
- Price range filter (min/max)
- Text search across product descriptions
- Sort by price or name (ascending/descending)
- Client-side language toggle (Spanish ↔ English) with localStorage persistence
- Footer with contact info, business hours, social links, and copyright
- Back-to-top button
- SEO meta tags and Open Graph / Twitter Card tags for social sharing
- Emoji favicon (💎)

## Color Scheme

- Primary accent: `rose` (soft pink) — hero gradient, filter highlights, prices, hover states
- WhatsApp button: `emerald` (green)
- Neutral: `gray` — backgrounds, text, borders
- Footer: `gray-800` dark background

## Categories

Products are categorized by `ref_code` prefix:
- `E` → Aretes (Earrings)
- `P` → Dijes (Pendants)
- `R` → Anillos (Rings)
